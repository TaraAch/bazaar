---
term_id: bhadrakali
title: "Bhadrakali"
related_terms: Kali, Devi, Shaktism, Hinduism
---
Bhadrakali refers to the mother goddess in one of her avatars. When the mother goddess takes on a ferocious form to stand up to injustice in the cosmos, she takes the form of Kali. Kali in a more benevolent and merciful mood is known as Bhadrakali.
