Balkrishna Vithaldas ("B.V.") Doshi was a renowned Indian architect who worked and was highly influenced by Le Corbusier and Louis Kahn. He is responsible for some of Ahmedabad's and the country's most iconic post-independence buildings. His architectural firm Vastu Shilpa is responsible for the Bhadra Plaza project.
