title: "Parsis"

---
Parsis are a community that migrated from Persia in the 7th century to the Indian subcontinent due to religious persecution. They are followers of the Zorashtrian faith. Since the community originally arrived in Gujarat, they consider Gujarati their native tongue although they speak a specific Parsi dialect.
