title: "Dargah"
related_terms: Sufis, Sufism, Pir
---
Dargah refers to a shrine or tomb belonging to a Sufi Saint. It is a site of worship and veneration and it is popularly believed that wishes are granted if you tie a thread or place a lock around the gates of the dargah. Dargahs appear in story 3,4 and 5 and are central to the sacral geography of the area.
