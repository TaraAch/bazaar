This was mentioned by Munir Kalimi in story 6 when he refers to the overall change in the socio-cultural environment which has in turn impacted the clientele at the depot. He speaks in coded terms but most who are familiar with contemporary Indian politics know that 1992 was a significant year that has shaped today's majoritarian climate. It refers to a wave of inter-communal riots that took place in various parts of India, in particular, the city of Mumbai. These riots were sparked by the demolition of a medieval mosque known as the 'Babri Masjid' by right-wing Hindu nationalist extremists who claimed it was originally the site of an ancient Hindu temple.
