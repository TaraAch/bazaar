term_id: sultanate
title: "Sultanate"
related_terms: Sultan
---
While the term more generally refers to the political territory that is under the rule of a Sultan, in this context, it refers to the Gujarat Sultanate which was established in 1394 by Muzaffar Shah after declaring independence from the Delhi Sultanate. The reigning Sultans of the Gujarat Sultanate are also collectively referred to as the Muzaffarid dynasty.
