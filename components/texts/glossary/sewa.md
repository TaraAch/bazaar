SEWA is an acronym that stands for the Self-Employed Women's Association which is an informal women workers' trade union. Founded in the city of Ahmedabad in 1972, it includes 3.2 million members across 18 states of India today. Deviben and Ratniben from story 3, as well as most of the women vendors in the market are SEWA members.
