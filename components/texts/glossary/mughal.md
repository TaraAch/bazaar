Mughal empire or Mughal dynasty refers to rulers of Turko-Mongol origins who ruled vast swathes of the Indian subcontinent from the early 16th to mid-18th century. In 1573, the Mughals under the rule of Emperor Akbar, gained control of Gujarat and the city of Ahmedabad from the reigning Sultan Muzaffar Shah II.
