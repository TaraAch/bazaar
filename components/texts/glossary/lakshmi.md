Goddess Lakshmi is the Hindu goddess of wealth and prosperity. She is also the consort of Vishnu, one of the three Gods that make up the Hindu triumvirate of Brahma, Vishnu and Shiva. Hindus worship her all year round but particularly during the festival of Diwali. In Gujarat, Diwali coincides with the new year.
