---
term_id: dabaan-ni-gaadi
title: "Dabaan ni Gaadi"
related_terms: AMC, Eviction
---
Dabaan ni gaadi refers to a Gujarati phrase which translates to "the eviction car". Dabaan literally translates to pressure- it refers to the coercive tactics deployed by the Municipality which includes circling the area in big vans or trucks in order to threaten street vendors and compel them to vacate the premises.
